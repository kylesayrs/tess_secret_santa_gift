Welcome to Tess' Guess!

In this game you must figure out the word from the prompt before you partner does.

You will be given the definition of a word from Urban Dictionary along with a word scramble.

First player to figure out the word can choose to make another player drink.

The first player to 5 correct words wins!

Remember to add terms in the edit tab.
